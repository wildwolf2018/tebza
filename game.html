The Menu Screen
1. To start new game press Play on the menu screen.
2. To play previously saved game, press Resume on menu screen.
3. To view high scores, press High Scores button on the menu screen.
4. To get help, press Help on the menu screen.
Note! The game uses cookies to store game state.
Choosing Modes
Change the game difficulty level by pressing easy or hard mode button on title screen.
Game Board
1. To move the player sprite, you first generate an arithmetic expression by pressing the Generate button.
2. The resulting expression will tell you by how many squares to move forward or backward.
3. Positive means moves forward and negative means move backward.
4. Click on the square you have chosen on the game board.
5. The game ends when the timer reaches 0 or you have reached the the square numbered 100.
6. If your total score is high enough, you can enter your username or any name to be displayed on the high scores screen.
